* Note: The N5250A PNA system is built upon the same application program/architecture as the 
        lower frequency PNA E836xA/B models, it just has higher frequency coverage/performance up to 
        110GHz standard or above (up to ~325GHz) with frequency extension options. In fact, if you query 
        the N5250A PNA instrument over remote GPIB interface/LAN, it will actually identify itself as 
        "E8361A", but show frequency coverage up to at least 110GHz (standard N5250 model). Do not be 
         alarmed, getting *IDN? query returned as "E8361A" is expected behavior when using the N5250A.
